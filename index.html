The Model: The Math

$$ \begin{aligned} &\frac{1}{P}\left\lbrack\frac{\partial\mathbf{v}}{\partial t} + (\mathbf{v}\cdot\nabla)\mathbf{v}\right\rbrack = -\nabla\overline{\omega} + R\theta\mathbf{\hat z} + \nabla^2\mathbf{v}, \label{rb1}\\ &\frac{\partial\theta}{\partial t} = v_z - \mathbf{v}\cdot\nabla\theta + \nabla^2\theta, \label{rb2}\\ &\nabla\cdot\mathbf{v} = 0, \label{rb3}\\ &\text{where}\\ &R \propto \Delta T \in \mathbb{R}, \hspace{1em} P \in \mathbb{R}, \hspace{1em} \overline{\omega} \sim \frac{p'}{\rho_0} \end{aligned} $$
